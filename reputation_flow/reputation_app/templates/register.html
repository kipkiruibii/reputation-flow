How it works

Connect Your Social Channels

Sync your business’s social media accounts with just a few clicks. Monitor reviews, mentions, and FAQs from customers across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and more—all in one place

Collect and Curate Social Proof

Automatically gather real-time customer feedback, reviews, and frequently asked questions. Organize and filter the most impactful comments to highlight positive experiences that build trust.

Display Customer Stories on Your Website

Seamlessly integrate the curated reviews and FAQs into your site. Showcase your customers’ words to create instant credibility and drive more conversions, all while engaging new and existing customers.

Other Features

Respond to Customers from One Dashboard - Engage with your audience effortlessly by replying to reviews, mentions, and FAQs directly from a single platform. No more juggling between multiple social channels—keep the conversation flowing and build customer relationships with ease.

Collect Valuable Customer Feedback - Go beyond reviews and gather detailed insights from your customers. Whether it's suggestions, concerns, or compliments, you’ll have a streamlined system to collect feedback and act on it, helping you continually improve your business.

Collaborate Efficiently with a Dedicated Team – Assign and manage tasks seamlessly by leveraging a skilled team dedicated to specific areas of your social channels. Each team member can focus on their unique responsibilities, ensuring tasks are handled efficiently and with expertise.
