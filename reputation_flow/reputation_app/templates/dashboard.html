You do not have any teams at the moment. To create a team:

    Ensure you are the admin/ have admin priviledges to continue

  1. Click the Create Team button at the top-right of this container

  2. Choose a name for the team and description of teams responsibility

  3. Give the necessary priviledges to the team

  4. Once done click finish. A new team will be created

  5. Locate the teams invite link (labeled as Teams Invite Link) at the top right of the container

  6. Share the invite link with your members.

  7. The members will be required to create an account after which they will be added to the team
